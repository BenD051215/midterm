A Little History...

As most already know, the rest of the NBA season was called off due to concers about the COVID-19. This comes at displeasure of many fans around the U.S. and the World. That being said, this upcoming season will hopefully be a nice revival to a once flourishing league. Since the 2019-2020 season has been postponed, the NBA urges fans to please stay safe during this epidemic and take extreme caution when surrounding yourself with a group of people. Here are some ways you can keep yourself safe from the virus....

Ways To Protect Yourself:

  • Wash your hands often
  • Keep your hands away from your face
  • Practice good personal hygiene
  • Use hand sanitizer
  • Cover your sneezes and coughs
  • Stay home if you're feeling even the slightest bit ill
  • Avoid large crowds
  • Do not shake hands with people
  • Clean areas that people come into contact with often
*We please urge you to take these the percautions into consideration* -NBA commisioner Adam Silver
